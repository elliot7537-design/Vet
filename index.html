About Us

A Family Clinic Built on Trust

Founded in 2009, PawCare Tijuana was born from a simple belief: pets on both sides of the border deserve excellent medical care at a price their families can afford. Our founder, Dr. Elena Ramírez, completed her veterinary training at UC Davis and returned to Tijuana with a mission to bring that standard of care home.

Today our team of 8 veterinarians and 20+ support staff serve pet owners from San Diego, Chula Vista, National City, and throughout Baja California. We are proud to bridge two communities with compassion, science, and a love for animals.
